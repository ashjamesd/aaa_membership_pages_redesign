Leading the AAA Membership Page Redesign Project

This project focused on the redesign and launch of four key AAA Western and Central New York membership pages: the Membership Overview Page, Membership Types & Benefits Page, Membership Product Page, and Gift Membership Page. Built on the foundation of the website modernization project, these redesigns aimed to create a clearer, more modern, and more accessible membership experience centered on value, trust, and ease of use.

Managing the Redesign Process

My role in this project was centered on project management and cross-functional coordination. I built timelines in Monday.com to organize the work, track progress, and keep the redesign moving across multiple phases and stakeholders. I also organized team meetings to review page iterations, collect feedback, and help drive alignment as the designs evolved.

Because this effort included several related membership pages rather than a single page update, maintaining structure and continuity across the project was especially important. The final redesigns introduced a more modern experience across all four pages while tailoring each one to a specific stage of the membership journey, from learning about benefits to joining or purchasing a gift membership.

Bridging Design and Development

A major part of my role was acting as the bridge between the team’s designer and web developer. With a background in full-stack development as well as experience working with front-end design tools such as Figma, I was able to translate technical backend documentation into design terms for the designer and then communicate design goals back into practical development terms for implementation.

This helped reduce friction between planning and execution and made it easier for the team to move from concept to launch. It also allowed the redesign process to stay grounded in both user experience goals and technical feasibility, which was especially valuable on a project that balanced design improvements, accessibility considerations, content strategy, and backend maintainability.

Project Outcomes

The redesigned pages were created to make AAA Membership easier to understand and easier to act on. Across the project, the new experiences focused on clarifying membership value, improving scanability, modernizing layouts, and creating stronger calls to action. The Membership Overview Page was reshaped to better serve prospective members, the Membership Types & Benefits Page became a more comprehensive and navigable source of benefit information, and the Membership Product and Gift Membership pages were redesigned to improve usability and trust during important conversion points.

Following launch, the project also included tracking page performance and user interaction to better understand how visitors engaged with the new experiences and whether the redesigns addressed issues identified in the earlier versions. This created a stronger foundation for future iteration and continued redesign work across the website.
